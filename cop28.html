COP28, Bhutan Pavilion

Goals

- Learn about Bhutan's comprehensive approach to carbon neutrality and decarbonization, including the Bhutan Climate Fund and energy transition initiatives.
- Understand how Bhutan's community-driven climate action programs, such as the De-suung movement, are driving positive change.
- Identify opportunities to collaborate with Bhutan or incorporate relevant elements of their climate strategy into her client projects.
- Stay up-to-date on the latest developments and announcements related to COP28 and Bhutan's climate endeavors.


Pain Points

- Finding concise, well-structured information about Bhutan's climate initiatives on the website.
- Navigating technical or financial details that may not be directly relevant to her consulting work.
- Ensuring she can effectively communicate Bhutan's unique cultural approach to climate action to her clients.
- Discovering case studies, data, and visuals that can be easily incorporated into her reports and presentations.
