UI/UX Design for Bouquet App

Goals

- Enable users to browse and select bouquets effortlessly, with categories like occasions (birthdays, anniversaries) or themes (romantic, sympathy).
- Allow users to customize bouquet options, like flower types, colors, and packaging, to make the gift more personalized.
- Provide live tracking for users to know the exact location and estimated delivery time of their bouquet.
- Streamline the checkout process with flexible delivery dates, same-day delivery options, and easy address entry.


Pain Points

- Users may feel frustrated if they cannot personalize the bouquet according to specific preferences.
- Users might get frustrated if delivery time estimates are unclear or if delivery is delayed.
- If the flowers arrive damaged or don’t meet the quality shown in the app, customers will be dissatisfied and may not order again.
- Users may miss opportunities to send flowers if they are not reminded of upcoming events like birthdays or anniversaries.
